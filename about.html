Our Story

Davis Boxing Club was founded on Omaha grit and a belief that boxing can unlock purpose for young people and families who need it most.

Why We Started

From neighborhood garages to a thriving community space, Davis Boxing Club began as a promise: provide a safe, disciplined environment where youth can feel seen, supported, and challenged to grow.

Founder Eric Hurley saw firsthand how boxing teaches more than footwork and jabs. It instills respect, sharpens awareness, and creates a sense of belonging. With roots deep in North Omaha, the club now serves youth and adults across the city, offering mentorship, academic support, and a healthy outlet when life gets heavy.

Today, every class is about more than training. It’s about building resilient leaders ready to champion their communities.

Our Mission

To create a safe, inclusive space where youth learn discipline, confidence, and purpose through boxing.

Core Values

  • Discipline — Show up, do the work, stay ready.
  • Respect — Honor yourself, your teammates, and the community.
  • Community — Lift as you climb; no one fights alone.
  • Growth — Every round is an opportunity to learn and improve.
  • Purpose — Fight for something bigger than yourself.
