Mission Snapshot

At Davis Boxing Club, we believe boxing builds more than athletes — it builds character, confidence, and community.

Empowering youth and underserved communities in Omaha, Nebraska through boxing — teaching discipline, confidence, awareness, and resilience that last a lifetime.

Why Davis Boxing Club?

Community Impact

Free and accessible training programs for Omaha youth, breaking down financial barriers to safe, structured mentorship.

Holistic Growth

Discipline, respect, and resilience are built into every round — helping athletes succeed in the ring and in life.

Inclusive Training

Programs designed for all ages, genders, and abilities with coaches committed to meeting athletes where they are.
